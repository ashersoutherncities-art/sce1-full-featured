Who We Are

Construction. Brokerage. Investment. Development.

Southern Cities Enterprises is a full-stack real estate company providing construction, brokerage, investor services, and development solutions across the Southeast. Our vertically integrated approach allows us to manage projects efficiently from acquisition and renovation to disposition and long-term ownership.

Frequently Asked Questions

What does Southern Cities Enterprises do?

Southern Cities Enterprises is a vertically integrated real estate company offering construction, brokerage, investor services, and development solutions. We help clients identify opportunities, execute projects, and maximize value across the entire real estate lifecycle.

Do you work with homeowners, investors, or partners?

We work with all three. Whether you're a homeowner looking to sell or renovate, an investor evaluating deals, or a partner exploring development opportunities, we provide tailored services to meet your needs.

Are your services provided by licensed professionals?

Yes. Our team includes licensed general contractors, real estate brokers, and experienced professionals. We maintain proper insurance, licensing, and compliance across all divisions.

Do you offer construction and brokerage services together?

Yes. Our integrated approach allows us to coordinate construction and brokerage seamlessly. This is especially valuable for fix-before-you-sell strategies, investor projects, and development deals.

Do you develop your own projects or partner with others?

Both. We develop our own projects and also collaborate with landowners, investors, and partners through joint ventures and strategic partnerships.

Do you guarantee investment returns or outcomes?

No. Real estate involves inherent risk, and we do not guarantee returns or specific outcomes. Our role is to provide strategic guidance, disciplined execution, and risk management support.

How do I know which division is right for my needs?

Contact us to discuss your goals. We'll evaluate your situation and recommend the best approach, whether that's a single service or a combination of divisions working together.

How do I get started?

Reach out through our contact form or email us directly. We'll schedule a conversation to understand your needs and determine how we can help.
