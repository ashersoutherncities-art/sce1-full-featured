Frequently Asked Questions

Is this community free to join?
Yes! Our Discord community is completely free. We believe in building a strong network where everyone can share knowledge and grow together.
Do I need to be an experienced investor?
Not at all. We welcome investors at all levels—from complete beginners to seasoned professionals. Our community is here to help you learn and grow.
What kind of deals are shared?
Members share wholesale deals, off-market properties, fix-and-flips, rental opportunities, land deals, and more. All focused on the Charlotte metro area.
How active is the community?
Very active! We have daily discussions, new deals posted regularly, and members online 24/7 to answer questions and share insights.
Can I promote my services?
We have designated channels for service providers and contractors who add value to the community. Self-promotion must be helpful and not spammy.
How do I get started?
Simply click the "Join Our Discord Community" button, create a Discord account if you don't have one, and introduce yourself in the welcome channel!
